Espresso

“Black as the devil, hot as hell, pure as an angel, sweet as love.” – Charles Maurice de Talleyrand

Esspresso Start

The perfect espresso starts with a good roast, grind, and press. Espresso is concentrated, pressurized coffee.

The Perfect Shot

After your make your brew, let it run for 30 seconds. Congrats! You have a single shot! The fluffy layer is called "crema".

Double Shot

For this, you need a larger cup to hold your coffee grinds. Then brew like you would a single shot.

Milk Foam

A byproduct of steamed milk. The key is to swirl the cup and feel the metal for temp.

Lets Make Art!

A latte is 1 part espresso, 2 parts steamed milk, 1 part milk foam. Latte art comes from the foam on top of crema.

Cappuccino

The gal is equal parts epresso, steamed milk, and milk foam. She's tricky- get her right or you'll get a latte!

Flat White

Epresso and steamed milk. There's no foam in this one, take care when steaming milk.

Latte Macchiato

Order is important here. First, add two parts steamed milk first! Then 1 shot of espresso.

Mocha

You ready? She goes: 1 part erpesso, 1 part hot coco, 1 part milk, 1 part foam!

Americano

She's plain. She simple. She's 1 part espresso, 3 parts hot water. A stronger coffee without that cuts some bitterness from espresso.
